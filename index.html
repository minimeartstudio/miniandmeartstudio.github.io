What is Mini & Me Art Studio?

Mini & Me Art Studio is a shared creative practice and living studio journal rooted in slow making, process art, and parent-child connection. Here, art isn’t about getting it right — it’s about slowing down, exploring freely, and creating side by side. There’s no pressure to perform or make something perfect. Just space to experiment, connect, and make memories together — because the moments matter more than the masterpiece.

Her freedom and instinct

Uninhibited mark-making, movement, experimentation, and the honest now.

My skill

Editing, layering, and composition — without erasing her voice.

Our art

Collaborative pieces that feel alive — raw and refined, held together in conversation.

About the Owner

Mini & Me grew out of Laura’s own return to art through motherhood — learning to slow down, create again, and see the world through little eyes. What began as making alongside her own child became a studio practice rooted in process, play, and presence. She believes in following a child’s lead, and creating an environment where both parent and child can explore, make a mess, and simply be together. Mini & Me is about more than painting — it’s about shared moments and building memories through art.

Our Process

Her marks become the raw layer. I respond with linework, editing, and quiet structure.
