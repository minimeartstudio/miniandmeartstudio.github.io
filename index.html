What is Mini & Me Art Studio?

Mini & Me Art Studio is a shared creative practice for parents and little ones. Art here isn’t about getting it right — it’s about making space to slow down, explore, and create side by side. There are no expectations to perform. No pressure to create something perfect. Only space to explore, experiment, and make memories side by side. Because the moments matter more than the masterpiece.

Her freedom

Uninhibited mark-making, movement, experimentation, and the honest now.

My skill

Editing, layering, composition, and quiet structure—without erasing her voice.

Our art

Collaborative pieces that feel alive—raw and refined, held together in conversation.

About the Owner

Mini & Me grew out of Laura’s own return to art through motherhood—learning to slow down, create again, and see the world through little eyes. What began as making alongside her own child became a studio practice rooted in process, play, and presence. She believes in process over perfection, in following a child’s lead, and in creating an environment where both parent and child can explore, make a mess, and simply be together. Mini & Me is about more than painting — it’s about shared moments, gentle guidance, and building memories through art.
